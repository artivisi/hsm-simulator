Help & Guidelines

What happens to my contribution?

Your passphrase is securely hashed and combined with other custodians' contributions to generate the master key. The original passphrase is never stored in plaintext.

What makes a good passphrase?

Use a long, random combination of words, numbers, and symbols. Avoid dictionary words, personal information, or patterns that could be guessed.

Is this secure?

This is an educational simulation. In production, contributions would be made using secure, out-of-band methods with proper verification and audit trails.
